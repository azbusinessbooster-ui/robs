About Rob's Painting AZ

A veteran-owned business providing superior painting services across the Phoenix Metropolitan area.

Rob's Painting AZ is a veteran-owned business that has been providing a complete line of commercial and residential painting services. Originally founded in Mesa, AZ, Rob's Painting AZ has expanded its coverage area to include all of the Phoenix Metropolitan area.

Our family of customers now spans from Gilbert to Surprise, from Queen Creek to Anthem, from Chandler to Paradise Valley and every community in between.

Rob's Painting AZ continues to grow in this difficult economic time by providing superior service. Their professional craftsmen pride themselves in providing a quality of service unmatched in their industry. Rob's Painting AZ offers written warranties on all of its work that equal or surpass any offered by other painting contractors.

Rob, Owner & Founder

Rob founded Rob's Painting AZ with a vision to provide unmatched quality and customer service in the painting industry. His commitment to excellence has made Rob's Painting AZ a trusted name throughout the Phoenix Metro area.
